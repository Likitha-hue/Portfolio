Overview

Rooted in System Thinking

The Brief
Custom Android Theme
Design a cohesive, fully tokenised Android UI theme that works across all native system screens — lock, home, dialler, messaging, settings — in both dark and light modes.
The Concept
Neon Glassmorphism
Layered translucent surfaces lit from within by primary neon green #00FF88, creating depth and atmosphere while maintaining full readability at all ambient conditions.
The Outcome
Dual-Mode System
A complete design system — colour tokens, two font families, 12 text styles, and 9 screen templates across both dark neon and light botanical modes.
Goals & Approach

What We Set Out to Build

Design Goals
Build a single token system that scales across 9+ native Android screens without breaking visual consistency.
Create a neon aesthetic that feels bold in dark mode but doesn't sacrifice legibility in high-ambient environments.
Define a dual-mode palette where light mode feels organic and botanical — not just an inverted dark.
Achieve WCAG AA contrast ratios on all text pairings in both colour modes.
Keep the system minimal enough for community customisation while opinionated enough to feel designed.
